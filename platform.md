## 1. Product Overview

**CityEyes** is a real-time landmark intelligence and navigation mobile application that helps tourists, drivers, and urban explorers locate verified landmarks around them.

Unlike traditional map apps that rely heavily on street names and structured addresses, CityEyes focuses on **human-recognizable landmarks** ‚Äî the way people actually give directions in cities.

Example:

> ‚ÄúTurn right after the Shell Filling Station.‚Äù
> ‚ÄúOpposite the Blue Gate Pharmacy.‚Äù

CityEyes makes these directions searchable, navigable, and shareable.

## 2. Problem Statement

In many cities ‚Äî especially across Africa and emerging markets:

* Addresses are inconsistent or non-standard.

* Tourists struggle with informal location systems.

* Deliveries fail due to vague address descriptions.

Traditional platforms like:

* Google Maps
